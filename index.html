Covid Testing Application

DxTerity

During the height of the Covid-19 pandemic I worked on a Covid testing application by DxTerity to track employee's status as they started returning to the office. This application allowed users to signup to receive tests at home and at the office then sent off and analyzed. The results would be accessible after registering for the site. I built out the registration process and various screens as well as debugging.

Technologies used include:

  • React
  • TypeScript
  • Redux
  • Styled Components
