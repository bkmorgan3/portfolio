About Me

My first experience building websites was in a web development class in high school. The class was interesting and I learned a lot but I didn’t take it very seriously. I was more focused on playing music and working with bands in some capacity.

It was a few years after college when I reconnected with an old friend and saw the sites he was building, full of animations and interactive content that I decided to refocus on web development. I found that dev work scratches the same itch as music for me, a perfect blend of technical skill and creativity. Now I totally flipped perspective where development is my main focus and music is a hobby.

When I'm away from my desk you’ll find me in dotwntown LA walking my dog, Luna, or in a neighborhood coffee shop enjoying time outside.
