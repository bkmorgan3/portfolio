About Me

My introduction to web development was in a high school where I built my first websites. I found it interesting and learned a lot, but at the time my main focus was music and working with bands.

A few years after college, I reconnected with an old friend who was creating sites filled with animations and interactive features, and it reignited my interest in web development. I realized that coding taps into the same creative and technical energy I loved in music—a balance of structure and expression. These days, development is my main focus and music has become my creative outlet.

When I’m away from my desk, you can usually find me walking my dog, Ramona, through downtown LA or relaxing at a neighborhood coffee shop.
