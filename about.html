About Brad

I had my first experience building websites was in high school but there wasn't an interest initially. It wasn't until a few years after that introduction when I saw web animations and interactive sites that I really had an interest to build cool things online.
I have always been pretty technical and creative through other avenues such as music and audio recording and building websites is now my primary outlet to bridge those traits.

Outside of tech work you can find me in downtown LA walking my dog or hear me playing bass with my band.
